This sample demonstrates the ability of Essential PDF to render Barcodes on PDF documents in the form of graphical objects. Essential PDF also has the support for exporting Barcode as image.

Essential PDF supports the following types of Barcodes.

  • Code39
  • Code39Extended
  • Code11
  • Codabar
  • Code32
  • Code93
  • Code93Extended
  • Code128A
  • Code128B
  • Code128C
  • DataMatrix
  • QRBarcode
  • GS1Barcode
  • UPC-ABarcode
