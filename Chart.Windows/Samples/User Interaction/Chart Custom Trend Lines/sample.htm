This sample demonstrates drawing custom trend lines in Essential Chart. It features drawing custom lines using the TrendLineAdder utility. By listening to the MouseDown and MouseUp events, trend lines can be generated. The color, border styles, symbols, and ToolTips of the trend lines are customizable.

Features:

  • Create trend lines- Custom lines are drawn by simply dragging the pointer over the chart area. This will trigger the MouseDown and MouseUp events to retrieve the start and end points of the trend lines. These points are added as data points of a line chart.
