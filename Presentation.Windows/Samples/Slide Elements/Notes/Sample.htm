This sample demonstrates how to add the Notes pages to a Presentation slide and how to convert the Notes pages in the PowerPoint Presentation to PDF document.

Features:

  • Create and modify the Notes pages in PowerPoint Presentation.
  • Convert the Notes pages in a PowerPoint Presentation to PDF document.

Note: This library supports creation, manipulation & conversion to PDF/image for .pptx format alone.
