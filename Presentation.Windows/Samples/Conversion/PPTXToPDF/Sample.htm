Essential Presentation provides support to convert the entire Presentation or a single slide to PDF with the help of Essential PDF. This Presentation to PDF Conversion sample demonstrates how to convert an existing Presentation to PDF using our library.

Features:

  • Supports Microsoft Office charts.
  • Converts Shapes along with text and fill styles.
  • Converts based on Master Shape and Slide Layouts.
  • Support Handouts Master.

Note: This library supports creation, manipulation & conversion to PDF/image for .pptx format alone.
