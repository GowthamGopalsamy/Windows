Essential Presentation has ability to convert the entire Presentation or single slide to image. This sample demonstrates how to convert the Presentation to an image using our API.

Features:

  • Supports Microsoft Office Chart to Image conversion.
  • Converts Shapes along with text and fill styles.
  • Converts based on Master Shape and Slide Layouts.

Note:This library supports creation, manipulation & conversion to PDF/image for .pptx format alone.
