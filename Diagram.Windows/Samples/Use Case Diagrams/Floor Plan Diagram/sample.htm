FloorPlan Diagram

This sample lets you to create a floor plan diagram with the symbols designed by Essential Diagram SymbolDesigner utility. This sample shows how to programmatically load a symbol palette file, generated by the SymbolDesigner, and how to use the SymbolModels, sourced from SymbolPalette to create the diagram symbols. You can easily design your floor plan diagram by dragging and dropping symbols from palette and connect them.
