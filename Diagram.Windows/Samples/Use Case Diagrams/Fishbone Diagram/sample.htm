Fishbone Diagram

This sample lets you to create a fishbone diagram with the symbols designed by Essential Diagram SymbolDesigner utility. This sample shows how to programmatically load a symbol palette file, generated by the SymbolDesigner, and how to use the SymbolModels, sourced from the SymbolPalette to create the diagram symbols. You can easily design your fishbone diagram by dragging and dropping the symbols from the palette and connect them.
