This sample showcases the built-in styles such as office 2010, office 2016 and metro that can be used to customize the default appearance of the pivot grid control.

Features:

Pivot grid for windows forms provides support for various visual styles:

  • Office 2010 Blue
  • Office 2010 Black
  • Office 2010 Silver
  • Office 2016 Black
  • Office 2016 White
  • Office 2016 Colorful
  • Office 2016 Dark Gray
  • Metro
